Spark Worldwide is a non-profit that opperates out of Burleson, Texas. Founded by Leanna Brewer in 1998, Spark has a heart for children around the world that are in need of resources, love, and opportunites. Spark is a ministry dedicated to children by funding orphanages, water wells, schools, missionaries and many support programs all around the world. Spark opperates in Africa, Belieze, Brazil, Columbia, Peru, Jamaca, and in the future many more countries.
